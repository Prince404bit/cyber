Security Features

End-to-End Encryption

All evidence, communications, and analysis results are encrypted with AES-256. Keys are managed through a secure HSM (Hardware Security Module).

Multi-Factor Authentication

Biometric verification, hardware tokens, and one-time passwords required for all system access. Session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity.

Chain of Custody

Digital chain of custody tracking with blockchain-inspired integrity verification. Tamper-evident logs for all evidence handling.

Role-Based Access Control

Granular permissions based on investigation role. Least privilege principle enforced. All access attempts logged and monitored.

Advanced Threat Protection

Real-time threat detection, intrusion prevention, and anomaly detection using AI-powered security monitoring.

Secure Data Retention

Automated backup with geo-redundancy. Retention policies compliant with legal requirements. Secure deletion with NIST standards.

Ethical Framework

Privacy Protection

All investigations respect privacy rights. Data minimization principles applied. Personal information redacted when not relevant to investigation.

AI Ethics & Bias Mitigation

Regular audits for algorithmic bias. Transparent AI decision-making processes. Human oversight required for all critical determinations.

Legal Compliance

Adherence to all relevant laws including GDPR, CCPA, and local evidence handling regulations. Regular legal reviews of procedures.

Accountability

Clear responsibility assignment. Regular third-party audits. Whistleblower protection for ethical concerns.

Transparency

Documentation of all analysis methods. Explanation of AI recommendations. Disclosure of system limitations.

Fairness & Justice

Equal treatment under the law. Protection against wrongful accusations. Support for exoneration when evidence permits.
